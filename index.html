About Me

Hi there! I'm Fang, a third-year undergraduate student majoring in Mathematics and Statistics at University of Michigan.

I'm currently working as an LLM Researcher in Statistics department, gratefully advised by Prof.Yixin Wang and Zhiwei Xu.

My research interests focus on investigating the weak-to-strong generalization capabilities of large language models and uncovering the fundamental laws that sustain their self-improvement.

As a former Data Analyst Intern at AstraZeneca, I led sales performance analytics and developed Power BI dashboards that streamlined therapy prioritization for over 30,000 healthcare institutions.

Research

Investigating Weak-to-Strong Generalization in Large Language Models

January 2023 - Present

Advisors: Prof. Yixin Wang and Zhiwei Xu

Exploring the fundamental mechanisms that enable large language models to generalize from weak to strong capabilities through self-improvement techniques.

  • Analyzing patterns in knowledge transfer across model scales
  • Developing metrics to quantify generalization capabilities
  • Investigating causal relationships in model learning trajectories

Statistical Properties of Self-Consistency in LLMs

September 2022 - December 2022

Studied how large language models maintain consistency in their outputs across different prompting techniques and evaluated statistical measures of reliability.

  • Developed framework for measuring self-consistency across diverse tasks
  • Identified correlations between consistency metrics and model performance
  • Presented findings at departmental research symposium
